Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Important Notice

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are generally considered final. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. General Policy

All purchases of digital subscriptions and keys are final. Once a key is activated or a subscription begins, it cannot be refunded. We strongly recommend testing our products through trial periods or free features when available.

2. Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If our software is completely non-functional and our team cannot resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice
  • Unauthorized Purchase: If your payment method was used without your consent (requires proof)

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Game bans or account suspensions
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to read product descriptions or requirements
  • Incompatibility with your system (check requirements before purchase)
  • Partial use of subscription time
  • Keys that have been activated
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Detection by anti-cheat systems

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

  1. Open a support ticket within 24 hours of purchase
  2. Provide your order details and reason for refund
  3. Include any relevant screenshots or proof
  4. Wait for our team to review your request (up to 72 hours)

5. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method or as store credit at our discretion. Processing time may vary depending on your payment provider.

6. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback without first contacting our support team will result in:

  • Immediate account termination
  • Permanent ban from all services
  • Blacklisting from future purchases

Please contact us first to resolve any payment issues.

7. Product Updates

We continuously update our products to maintain functionality. Temporary downtime for updates does not qualify for refunds. We strive to minimize disruption and provide timely updates.

8. Reseller Purchases

If you purchased through an authorized reseller, please contact them directly for refund requests. We are not responsible for refunds on purchases made through third-party resellers.

9. Contact

For refund inquiries, please open a support ticket or contact us through our official Discord server. Include your username and purchase details for faster processing.
